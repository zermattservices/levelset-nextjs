# Welcome to Levelset

Levelset is a comprehensive team performance and management platform designed specifically for restaurant operations. It provides tools for tracking employee performance, managing discipline, and optimizing your team's development.

## What is Levelset?

Levelset helps restaurant leaders:

- **Track Positional Excellence** - Rate team members on position-specific criteria to identify strengths and growth opportunities
- **Manage Discipline** - Document and track infractions with a structured progressive discipline system
- **Maintain Your Roster** - Keep employee information, roles, and statuses up to date
- **Configure Your Organization** - Customize positions, rating criteria, roles, and more
