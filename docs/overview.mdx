# Welcome to Levelset

Levelset helps you track team performance, manage discipline, and develop your employees. This guide will show you how to use every feature.

## What Can You Do With Levelset?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Rate Team Members" icon="star" href="/positional-excellence/overview">
    Submit positional ratings to track how your team performs in different roles
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track Discipline" icon="clipboard-list" href="/discipline/overview">
    Document infractions and follow a consistent discipline process
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage Your Roster" icon="users" href="/roster-management/overview">
    Keep employee information, roles, and status up to date
  </Card>
  <Card title="Configure Settings" icon="gear" href="/organization-settings/overview">
    Customize positions, criteria, roles, and permissions for your organization
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Go to [app.levelset.io](https://app.levelset.io) and sign in with your email and password, or use Google Sign-In.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Location">
    If you have access to multiple locations, select the one you want to work with from the dropdown in the navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Using Levelset">
    Navigate to any section using the menu at the top of the screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Main Sections

| Section | What It's For |
|---------|---------------|
| **Home** | See an overview of your team's performance metrics |
| **Positional Excellence** | View ratings and track employee progress |
| **Discipline** | Manage infractions and disciplinary actions |
| **Roster** | View and edit employee information |
| **Settings** | Configure how Levelset works for your organization |
