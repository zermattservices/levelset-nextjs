# First Login

This guide walks you through your first login to Levelset and helps you get oriented with the platform.

## Accessing Levelset

### Web Dashboard

Access the Levelset dashboard at:
- **Production:** [app.levelset.io](https://app.levelset.io)
- **Preview/Testing:** [preview.levelset.io](https://preview.levelset.io)

### Mobile App (PWA)

Leaders can also access certain features through the mobile Progressive Web App. Ask your administrator for the QR code or direct link to install the app on your device.

## Signing In

### Email and Password

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your temporary password (provided by your administrator)
4. Click **Sign In**

![Levelset login page showing email and password fields with Sign In button](/images/screenshots/login-page.png)

<Note>
  If you received a temporary password, you'll want to change it after your first login for security.
</Note>

### Google Sign-In

If your email is a Google or Google Workspace account, you can also sign in using the **Sign in with Google** button for a faster login experience.

## Exploring the Dashboard

After signing in, you'll land on the home dashboard. Here's what you'll see:

### Home Page

The home page provides an overview of key metrics:

- **360 Overview** - High-level performance indicators
- **Operational Excellence** - Metrics for guest experience areas

![Levelset home dashboard showing 360 Overview with Positional Excellence and Discipline Points](/images/screenshots/dashboard-home.png)

### Selecting a Location

If you have access to multiple locations:

1. Look for the location dropdown in the top navigation
2. Click to see available locations
3. Select the location you want to view

Your selection will persist as you navigate through the platform.

## Key Areas to Explore

<Steps>
  <Step title="Positional Excellence">
    View team member ratings across different positions. See the Overview tab for a summary, or dive into Employee Ratings for detailed breakdowns.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Discipline Dashboard">
    If enabled for your organization, view and manage discipline records. Track infractions and see recommended actions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Roster">
    View and manage your team roster. Update employee information, roles, and status.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Organization Settings">
    Configure positions, rating criteria, and other organizational settings (requires appropriate permissions).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mobile Features

The mobile PWA provides quick access to commonly-used features for leaders:

- **Submit Ratings** - Rate team members on positional criteria
- **Document Infractions** - Record discipline incidents
- **View PEA Classic** - See the classic positional excellence view

<Tip>
  Add the PWA to your home screen for quick access. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" option in your mobile browser.
</Tip>

## Need Help?

If you're having trouble logging in or navigating the platform:

1. Check that you're using the correct email address
2. Try the "Forgot Password" link if available
3. Contact your organization's Levelset administrator
4. Reach out to [support@levelset.io](mailto:support@levelset.io)
