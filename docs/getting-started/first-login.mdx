# First Login

This guide walks you through logging in to Levelset and getting oriented.

## How to Log In

### Step 1: Go to Levelset

Open your web browser and go to **[app.levelset.io](https://app.levelset.io)**

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

You have two options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password">
    1. Enter your email address
    2. Enter your password (your administrator should have provided this)
    3. Click **Sign In**
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Google Sign-In">
    1. Click **Sign in with Google**
    2. Select your Google account
    3. You'll be logged in automatically
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
If you don't have login credentials, contact your administrator to have an account created for you.
</Note>

### Step 3: Select Your Location

If you have access to multiple locations:

1. A modal will appear asking you to select a location
2. Choose the location you want to work with
3. Click to confirm

You can change locations later using the dropdown in the navigation bar.

## What You'll See After Logging In

### Home Page

The home page shows an overview of your team's performance:

- **Positional Excellence metrics** - Average ratings and trends
- **Discipline metrics** - Current points and recommended actions

Click on any metric card to go to that section for more details.

### Navigation

Use the menu at the top to access different sections:

| Menu Item | What It Shows |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Home** | Performance overview dashboard |
| **Positional Excellence** | Team ratings by position |
| **Discipline** | Infraction records and actions |
| **Roster** | Employee list and details |

### Your Profile

Click your profile icon (top right) to:

- View your name and role
- Access **Organization Settings** (if you have permission)
- **Log out** when you're done

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    Contact your organization's administrator. They can reset your password or create a new one.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="My email isn't recognized">
    Make sure you're using the exact email address that was set up for your account. Check with your administrator if you're unsure.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Google Sign-In isn't working">
    - Make sure you're selecting the correct Google account
    - Your Google account email must match the email in your Levelset user account
    - Try signing in with email/password instead
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I can log in but can't see any data">
    You may need to select a location. Look for a modal or dropdown prompting you to choose a location.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
