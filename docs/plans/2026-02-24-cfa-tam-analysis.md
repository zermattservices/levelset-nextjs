# Chick-fil-A Total Addressable Market (TAM) Analysis

**Date:** February 24, 2026
**Data Source:** CFA Location List (November 11, 2025)
**Prepared for:** Levelset Strategic Planning

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Chick-fil-A operates approximately 2,900 locations across the United States (with a small international presence), managed by approximately 2,286 unique Operators under a single-brand franchise model. The brand is experiencing accelerating unit growth, averaging 172 new openings per year over the last three years, with 2025 on pace as the highest-growth year on record.

Levelset's operational excellence platform is purpose-built for the Chick-fil-A ecosystem. At full market penetration with blended pricing of $162/location/month, the CFA TAM represents approximately **$5.68M in annual recurring revenue** today, growing to an estimated **$6.31M ARR by end of 2027**. Multi-unit Operators controlling 40% of all locations present an efficient beachhead strategy for initial go-to-market.

## 6. Strategic Insights

### 6.1 Multi-Unit Operators as Initial Target

The ~550 multi-unit Operators managing ~1,164 locations (40.1% of the system) are the ideal beachhead:

- **Higher urgency:** Cross-location consistency is harder to maintain manually
- **Better unit economics:** Fewer sales relationships per location acquired
- **Expansion built in:** Operators frequently add locations, growing their Levelset footprint organically
- **Proof of concept:** Success with multi-unit Operators provides compelling case studies for single-unit Operators

### 6.2 Geographic Concentration Enables Efficient GTM

The top 5 states (TX, GA, FL, CA, NC) account for 45.4% of all locations (1,318 units). A regionally-focused sales strategy can reach nearly half the market with concentrated effort:

- **Texas alone** represents 15.7% of the total market (455 locations)
- **Southeast corridor** (GA, FL, NC, SC, AL, TN) accounts for 32.8% (952 locations)
- Regional field representatives can provide hands-on onboarding and support

### 6.3 FSU Dominance Simplifies Product Fit

With 85.4% of locations being Free-Standing Units, Levelset can optimize its feature set for the FSU operational model first, then adapt for Mall, DTO, and Satellite formats as secondary priorities. FSU locations have the most complex operational needs (full kitchen, dining room, drive-thru, catering) and stand to benefit most from Levelset's platform.

### 6.4 Growth Trajectory Expands TAM Annually

At the current 3-year average of 172 new locations per year (and accelerating), the TAM grows by approximately $332K annually at blended pricing. Every new CFA location is a potential Levelset customer from day one. New Operators setting up their first location are particularly receptive to technology solutions that help them establish operational rigor early.

### 6.5 Single-Brand Focus as Competitive Advantage

Unlike multi-brand restaurant technology platforms, Levelset's exclusive focus on the CFA ecosystem provides:

- **Deep product-market fit:** Features built specifically for CFA operational patterns (PEA ratings, CARES infractions, shift setups)
- **Network effects:** As adoption grows, cross-Operator benchmarking and best-practice sharing become increasingly valuable
- **Defensible moat:** Deep integration with CFA-specific workflows creates high switching costs
