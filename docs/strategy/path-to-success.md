## Phase 3: Post-Approach Conversion (April 2026)

**Goal: Convert Approach leads into paying customers.**

### Immediate Follow-Up (Days 1-3)
- Personalized email to every lead within 24 hours
- Reference specific conversation topics from the booth
- Include direct link to free 30-day Pro trial
- Offer personal onboarding call

### Nurture Sequence (Weeks 1-4)
- Email drip campaign (if CRM/email system built):
  1. Day 0: Thank you + trial link
  2. Day 3: "Here's what operators like you are doing with Levelset" (case study)
  3. Day 7: "3 things you can do in your first week" (quick wins)
  4. Day 14: "How's the trial going?" + offer onboarding call
  5. Day 21: "Your trial is almost up" + conversion offer
- If CRM not built: Manual email follow-up using the same cadence

### Conversion Target
| Metric | Target | Revenue Impact |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Trials started | 10+ | - |
| Conversions (trial → paid) | 5+ | $1,245-14,940/mo |

## Key Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Can't ship everything before The Approach** | Reduced demo impact | Prioritize: PE + Discipline + Scheduling + Development Plans. These tell the connected story. |
| **Low conversion from event leads** | Slow revenue start | Over-invest in follow-up. Personalized, not automated. Make onboarding effortless. |
| **Solo founder burnout** | Product velocity drops | Prioritize ruthlessly. Use AI tools aggressively. Don't build what's not needed yet. |
| **Customer churn** | Revenue loss + negative word of mouth | Over-invest in customer success. Every churned customer is a lost reference. |
| **CFA corporate pushback** | Market access restricted | Position as an independent vendor (like HotSchedules). Not a CFA corporate tool. |
| **Competitor enters market** | Market share pressure | Move fast. Deep CFA integration is the moat. First-mover advantage matters. |
| **Pricing too high for single-unit operators** | Limits addressable market | Core tier at $99 provides an entry point. Convert to Pro as they see value. |
